Depth of Field

Though it may sound like something thats complicated itâ€™s really not. Depth of Field is just a way of saying how much of the image is in focus. Do you want to be able to see everything in the photo? Or do you just one thing to be in focus?

Depth of field is controlled by your cameras aperture. (you can learn more on aperture here) The wider the aperture the shallower the depth of field, and less of the image will be in focus. Vice versa, the smaller the aperture the longer depth of field you have and more of the image will be in focus.
