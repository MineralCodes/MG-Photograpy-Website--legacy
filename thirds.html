Rule of Thirds

The rule of thirds is a compositional tool photographers use to place objects in a photo. It uses a 3 by 3 grid and objects are placed using this grid. Important objects will be placed on one or more of the 4 intersection points, and the horizon will be lined up with one of the two vertical lines.
