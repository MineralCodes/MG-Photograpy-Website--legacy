Camera Types

There are many different camera types, but for the sake of keeping it simple we’ll talk about the three most common ones. You have your phone’s camera, your basic Point and Shoot, and then you have a DigitalSLR

First there is the phone camera is the most basic camera there is, and just about everyone has one. These produce lower quality images than other cameras, but they’re getting better all the time.

Next you have the basic Point and Shoot. These will usually be small enough to fit in your pocket and most of the time the will produce higher quality images than a phone camera. Also you have a little bit more control over your settings.

Last you have your Digital Single Lens Reflex (DSLR for short). These are more expensive than Point and Shoot but they allow you t take full control over your settings. They also produce higher quality images than Point and Shoot camera.
